About State Sips

State Sips is an app for beer lovers in the United States. Using the app is easy, just click on a state to list its available breweries.

This app is developed as the final project for the course “Frontend-baserad Webbutveckling, DT211G” at Webbutvecklingsprogrammet, Mittuniversitetet.
